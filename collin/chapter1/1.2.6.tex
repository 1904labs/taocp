\section*{Definition}
\[
  \dbinom{n}{k} = \dfrac{n!}{k!(n-k)!} = \dfrac{n(n-1)...(n-k+1)}{k(k-1)...(1)}.
\]

For example,

\[
  \dbinom{5}{3} = \dfrac{5 \cdot 4 \cdot 3}{3 \cdot 2 \cdot 1} = 10,
\]
